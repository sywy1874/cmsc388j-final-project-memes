MEME-STAGRAM

Project Members: Syam Angalakuditi, Eric Yi, Raymond Yu

This website was created for sharing memes. Once registered, users can log in to post their own memes to the site. Memes will be assigned a category representing meme types (such as wholesome, surreal, shitpost, etc). Some features include a commenting system, searching for memes, searching for users, looking at your account stats (plotly). When searching for a user, they can see all the posts that the former has made. Additionally using flask_mail a welcome email is sent to the user when they register!
